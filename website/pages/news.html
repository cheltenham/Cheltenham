Bridge

We have about 10 women in our bridge group and would love to have more. We play in the evenings, starting at about 7pm. Many are beginners, so no one should feel shy about coming.

We'd also like to get a daytime group started if we could get enough interested players. We play once per month, rotating hostesses. The hostess chooses the day. We usually drink wine and have a good time -- no serious playing.

If you're interested in playing bridge with us, call Mary Rose at 439-2672 or send an email to mrcomeo@yahoo.com.
